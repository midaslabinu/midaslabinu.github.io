Undergraduate researcher **Jun Young Lee** and colleagues published the paper **“Adaptive Sequence-to-Sequence Learning for Long-Term Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor Temperature Prediction”** in *AIP Advances*.

The study proposes sequence-to-sequence learning methods for accurate and stable long-term prediction of permanent magnet temperature.

The proposed models incorporate adaptive normalization, probabilistic teacher forcing, and an attention mechanism to improve long-horizon prediction performance.
