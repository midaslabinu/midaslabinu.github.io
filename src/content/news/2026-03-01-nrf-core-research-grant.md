MIDAS Lab was awarded a **Core Research grant under the Excellent Research Program** of the National Research Foundation of Korea.

- **Research period:** March 1, 2026 – February 28, 2029
- **Project number:** RS-2026-25483035

The project will support the laboratory’s research on artificial intelligence, bioinformatics, biomedical data analysis, and computational methods for understanding complex biological systems.
