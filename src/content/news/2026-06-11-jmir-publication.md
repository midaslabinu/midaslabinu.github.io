PhD student **Arifa Zahir** and colleagues published the paper **“A Machine Learning Approach to Voice-Based Parkinson Disease Screening Using Multiview Spectrogram and Speech Recognition Features: Diagnostic Study”** in *JMIR Medical Informatics*.

The study presents a machine learning framework for noninvasive Parkinson disease screening using multiple spectrogram representations and speech-recognition-based features.

The proposed model combines Mel spectrogram, short-time Fourier transform, and constant-Q transform representations with a recognition-aware feature derived from automatic speech recognition.
