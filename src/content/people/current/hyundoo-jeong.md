Dr. Hyundoo Jeong is an Associate Professor in the Department of Biomedical & Robotics Engineering, College of Engineering, Incheon National University. He leads the Machine Intelligence and Data Science Laboratory (MIDAS Lab).

His research focuses on computational methods for bioinformatics, network biology, single-cell and spatial transcriptomics, healthcare AI, and AI-driven drug discovery.

## Education

- **Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering**, Texas A&M University, College Station, USA, 2017
- **M.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science**, Seoul National University, Seoul, Republic of Korea, 2009
- **B.S. in Electronic and Electrical Engineering, Summa Cum Laude**, Inha University, Incheon, Republic of Korea, 2007
