Jinwoo Park is an undergraduate student majoring in Mechatronics Engineering at Incheon National University, with an academic background in mechanical and electronic engineering.
At MIDAS Lab, he conducts research on contactless sensing using Wi-Fi Channel State Information (CSI). His current work applies deep learning models, including CNNs and LSTMs, to publicly available CSI datasets for human activity recognition and vital sign monitoring.
He is particularly interested in benchmarking standardized models across multiple public datasets and developing reproducible and generalizable sensing methods for real-world applications.


## Research Interests

- WiFi Sensing
- Machine Learning/Deep Learning
- LLM Applications
