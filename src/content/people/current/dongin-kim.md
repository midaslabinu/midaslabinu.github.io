Dongin Kim received his M.S. degree in Biomedical Sciences with a specialization in Biomedical Informatics from Ajou University and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree at MIDAS Lab, Incheon National University. He specializes in bridging genomics, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. His research focuses on biological AGI, cell signaling, and in silico modeling, with an emphasis on transforming complex biological datasets into controllable, production-grade AI models and clinical-grade computational pipelines.
