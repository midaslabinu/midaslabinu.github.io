Arifa Zahir received her M.S. degree from COMSATS University Islamabad, Pakistan, and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Bio-Robot Systems Engineering at the Machine Intelligence & Data Science Lab, Incheon National University. Her research focuses on bioinformatics and artificial intelligence for biological data analysis. She is particularly interested in single-cell data analysis, machine learning, and the development of AI-driven computational methods for addressing challenges in bioinformatics and biomedical research.

- Single-Cell Data Analysis
